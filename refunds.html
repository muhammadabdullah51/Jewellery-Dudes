Return and Exchange Policy

At Jewellery Dudes, your satisfaction is our top priority. We offer a 30-day return and exchange policy to ensure you are happy with your purchase. If you are not completely satisfied, you may return or exchange your item within 30 days of the purchase date, provided it is in its original condition, unworn, and includes all original packaging and tags. To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our customer service team with your order details, and they will guide you through the process. Whether you want a refund or a different product, we strive to make the experience as smooth and hassle-free as possible.

Return Policy

At Jewellery Dudes, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not, you may return your item within 30 days of the purchase date for a full refund or exchange. The item must be in its original condition, unworn, and with all original packaging and tags. To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team for further instructions.

Repair Policy

We understand that jewelry can sometimes require repairs. Jewellery Dudes offers a complimentary repair service for any manufacturing defects within the first year of purchase. For any other repairs outside of this period or due to wear and tear, we offer repair services at a reasonable fee. Please contact our customer service team with details and photos of the damage, and we will guide you through the repair process.

Refund Policy

Jewellery Dudes is committed to ensuring your complete satisfaction with your purchase. We offer a 30-day return and exchange policy for items in their original condition. For manufacturing defects, we provide complimentary repairs within the first year. Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. Please note that shipping costs are non-refundable. For exchanges, contact our customer service team to initiate the process. Our goal is to provide a hassle-free and pleasant shopping experience, ensuring you are delighted with your jewelry purchase.

Exchange Policy

If you wish to exchange an item, we are happy to accommodate your request within 30 days of purchase. The item must be in its original condition, unworn, and with all original packaging and tags. To begin the exchange process, please contact our customer service team with your order details and the item you wish to exchange for. We will guide you through the steps to ensure a smooth exchange experience.
