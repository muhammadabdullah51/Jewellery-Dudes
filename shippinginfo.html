Shipping Information

Our goal is to provide a seamless and efficient shipping experience for all our customers. We offer various shipping options to cater to your needs, ensuring your precious jewelry arrives safely and promptly. Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days, and you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order has been dispatched.

How to Track Your Order

Tracking your order is simple and convenient. Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email containing your tracking number and a link to the courierâ€™s website. You can use this information to monitor the status and location of your package in real-time, ensuring you know exactly when to expect your beautiful new jewelry.

Shipping Costs

We strive to offer competitive and transparent shipping rates. Shipping costs are calculated based on the destination, shipping method selected, and the weight of the items purchased. During the checkout process, you will see the total shipping cost before finalizing your purchase, so there are no surprises.

International Shipping

We proudly offer international shipping to bring our exquisite jewelry to customers around the globe. Please note that international shipments may be subject to additional customs fees, duties, and taxes imposed by the destination country. These charges are the responsibility of the customer, and we recommend checking with your local customs office for more information.

Shipping Policy

At Jewellery Dudes, we are committed to ensuring that your purchase reaches you in perfect condition and in a timely manner. We process orders within 1-2 business days and provide various shipping options to suit your needs. Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a tracking number to monitor its progress. Shipping costs are transparent and calculated at checkout. For our international customers, please be aware that additional customs fees may apply. We aim to deliver your jewelry safely, no matter where you are in the world.
