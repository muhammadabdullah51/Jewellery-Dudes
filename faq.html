#FAQ

Got Questions? We've Got Answers: Your Go-To Guide for All Things Jewellery Dudes

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of jewelry do you offer?

We offer a wide range of jewelry, including rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings, and custom-designed pieces to suit every occasion and style.

Do you provide custom jewelry design services?

Yes, we specialize in custom jewelry design. Our expert designers work closely with you to create unique, personalized pieces that reflect your vision and style.

What materials are used in your jewelry?

We use high-quality materials, including gold, silver, platinum, and a variety of precious and semi-precious gemstones, ensuring durability and lasting beauty.

How can I determine my ring size?

You can use our online ring size guide or visit our store for a professional fitting. We also offer free resizing for rings purchased from us.

How can I contact customer service for further assistance?

You can reach our customer service team via email at support@[jewelrystore.com] or by calling our toll-free number at 1-800-123-4567. We're here to help with any questions or concerns you may have.

Do you offer jewelry cleaning and repair services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive cleaning and repair services to keep your jewelry looking its best. Visit our services page for more details.

What is your return and exchange policy?

We offer a 30-day return and exchange policy on most items. Custom and personalized pieces are not eligible for returns. Please see our return policy page for full details.

How long does it take to receive a custom jewelry piece?

Custom jewelry typically takes 4-6 weeks to design and create, depending on the complexity of the piece. We will keep you updated throughout the process.

Are your gemstones ethically sourced?

Absolutely. We are committed to ethical sourcing and ensure that all our gemstones are conflict-free and sourced from reputable suppliers.

Do you offer financing or payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options and payment plans to make your purchase more manageable. Please contact our customer service for more information.
